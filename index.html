Tally ARS is a live audience response system. Attendees join with a code or QR scan from their own phone — no app install, no account.

Build your questions, start a session, and open one question at a time. Votes tally live on the host dashboard. When you're done, end the session to generate a formal report and export your data.

Sessions are temporary — once a session ends, its data is exported to your device and removed from the live system. Import a past export any time to reuse its questions, with the option to compare against prior results.
